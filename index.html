About Me

Hello! I’m Hasan Al-Saafen, a fourth-year Computer Science student at Palestine Polytechnic University (PPU) with a strong passion for front-end development. I specialize in building responsive, user-centered web interfaces using modern tools like React, Tailwind CSS, and Redux. I also have a solid foundation in back-end development, including Node.js, Express, and RESTful APIs. Over the past few months, I’ve completed multiple professional certifications from IBM and Coursera in areas such as cloud computing, DevOps, and full-stack web development. My goal is to create clean, accessible, and performance-optimized web experiences that blend design and functionality seamlessly. Currently, I’m working on a smart educational platform for my graduation project, and I’m actively involved in Agile-based team projects that integrate real-world software development practices.
